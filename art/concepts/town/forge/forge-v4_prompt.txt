Revised: Pixel art showcasing a medieval fantasy blacksmith's forge building on a 1024x1024 native resolution. Designed in a shallow three-quarter orthographic perspective, it unveils the front wall and left side wall, plus some of the roof. The building itself is comprised of a short, asymmetrical stone base made of irregular warm leather-brown and dark steel-grey cobbles. A wide, door-less rectangular entrance exists, from which a warm ember-orange light emanates, casting a low dusk-lit hue on the surrounding cobblestone area. The building features a tall, eccentric brick chimney that rises off-center from the roofline, hinting at pluming smoke. The roof is dark slate streaked with wooden, leather-brown ribs. A deep violet night sky looms behind in contrast with the cool surrounding apart from the glowing doorway. Effectively, the palette is restrained to approximately 14 colors, themed around a warm forge against a cool dusk. We're aiming for flat shading, crisp pixel edges without any traces of anti-aliasing, gradients, or blur. This concept draws some inspiration from Moonlighter's shop exterior and Graveyard Keeper's village buildings, albeit it stays clear from featuring any characters, NPCs, or smoke particles. The entire scene is set on a neutral charcoal background, with the building isolated in the center with a roughly 10% margin. The art should ignore any modern elements and maintain a purely medieval aesthetic.
